Our Story

Hello, my name is Shana.

I am the business owner of Shizuku. I was born and raised in Okinawa, Japan. I can call myself a proud Okinawan citizen who loves to represent the beautiful Okinawan mountains, beach, and heritage. My love for nature has set me on the path to create this business where I can protect the ecology of Okinawa since our beautiful ocean and mountains are in danger due to climate change and local construction. I feel the strong need to preserve our beautiful island, and I hope you also want to join my journey through supporting my business. Thank you!
