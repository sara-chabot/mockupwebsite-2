We Give Back to Our Community

We are proud of our commitments to the local community. Starting 2015, Shizuku has launched a campaign project to donate back to the local Okinawan non-profit organizations who support the coral reef restoration. Every time a customer purchases our product, 15% of our revenue will be proceeded to these organization.
