OUR MISSION

Hello, we are Shizuku. We create accesories in Okinawa, Japan. Our mission is to give back to nature and community through our accessories. All of our products are ethically sourced and is traceable; we we want to create a future where ethical sourcing will be a normal practice. We hope you join our journey together by supporting our business and mission.

OUR GOALS

Our main goal at Shizuku has always been giving back to the community. We launched a project in 2022 where we will collect donations through your purchase in which 5% will be proceeded to our donation funds. By 2023, we would like to give the non-profit organization that supports coral reef restoration a total of 200,000 Yen.
